Overview | SMK Tunku Ampuan Durah & UiTM Campus Rembau



"SMK Tunku Ampuan Durah is my previous high school which contains a lot of memories about my friends, teachers and many more. The school is located at Negeri Sembilan, Seremban and it is well known school by people in Negeri Sembilan. When I was studied there, I learn many things from my teachers. We can say like from nothing to something. There are so kind and supportive and it makes me felt comfortable to study. Furthermore, I also got promoted by my islamic club teacher to be a president of the club. Yeah people may think it is just a normal club, but I personally think that it is a good club where we can learn good things there"

"UiTM Campus Rembau is the place where I currently doing my diploma in Information Management. The place is very good and comfortable to study. The staff and the organization are well organized and I don't have any problems study here. To be honest, I'm really grateful to be chosen to study at UiTM because we know that many knowledgeble and succussfull people were born from UiTM also the university were created special to help Malay people and Bumiputera to continue their studies. I think it's enough for me to be grateful to be chosen as a students in UiTM"
